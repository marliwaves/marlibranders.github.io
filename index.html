I Am Different

I am 19 years old and live in Cape Town, South Africa.
From the outside my life might look ordinary,
some may even say uninspiring.
Loving family, exceptional school and almost no difficulties at all.
But I always knew I was different. I can see things that others are apparently missing. I can see the hurt and the pain in this world. And I can see that very few are doing anything about it.
I believe in compassion

It is a vital part of our human nature that many live without. If selfishness was classified as a sickness, our world would be fatally ill.
What are the symptoms you ask?

According to Global Issues the poverty stricken 40% of the world’s population only accounts for 5% of global income. The wealthiest 20% accounts for three-quarters of world income.
Don't get me wrong: I am not trying to antagonise those who have been blessed with money. On the contrary I believe they were blessed with a purpose, because they will be able to help those who cannot help themselves.
I am a Christian and I believe the best way to spread the gospel is by being an example of Jesus’ love. And the only way to reach those who live in poverty is to provide in their basic needs.
All my efforts would be in vain

without help from above and therefore I have prayed and asked Father God for guidance as I take my first step of action. I firmly believe He has lead me to attend a Discipleship Training School at Youth With A Mission (YWAM) Yosemite in 2016.
A DTS course can be divided into two sections mainly referred to as the Lecture Phase and the Outreach Phase. During the three month Lecture Phase we will be equipped with Bible knowledge and taught basic ministry skills at the YWAM Yosemite location. The three month Outreach Phase will be used to put our newly founded skills and knowledge to the test as we will be spending it at one of the many YWAM locations around the world where our help is most needed.
Why America?

Upon browsing locations, YWAM Yosemite caught my eye. At first I thought it was a ridiculous idea, mainly because it is so far from home and will be much more expensive. But through many confirmations I believe that this is the right direction for my life and I am willing to take a leap of faith.
Here I will be able to expand my skills of ministry and make a difference through outreach programs. Unfortunately this endeavour will cost money and therefore I am asking all those who share my vision out there to help me make a difference by donating any amount possible.
